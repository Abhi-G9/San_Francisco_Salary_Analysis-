# 📋 Business Questions

The following business questions were explored during the exploratory data analysis (EDA) of the San Francisco Employee Salaries dataset.

1. Which employee has the highest Base Pay?

2. How are employee salaries distributed?

3. What is the average Base Pay of all employees?

4. What is the average Base Pay for each year (2011–2014)?

5. Who are the highest-paid employees in each year (2011–2014)?

6. Which are the top 5 highest-paying job titles?

7. Which job title has the maximum employees?

8. Which job titles receive the highest overtime pay?

9. How many employees did not receive any overtime pay?

10. Who are the top 10 overtime earners?

11. Which employees have the lowest Total Pay & Benefits?

12. Who are the employees with exceptionally high total compensation?

13. Which employees have overtime pay lower than their Benefits?

14. Which employees receive Benefits despite having zero Base Pay?

15. Which employees receive Base Pay but no Benefits?

16. Which employees receive Other Pay but no Overtime Pay?
